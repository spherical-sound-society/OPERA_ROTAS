HOW TO UPDATE FIRMWARE

You need to have a st link vs gadget, that looks like this:

https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/I/41d2CK0iTtL._AC_.jpg

And costs 2-3USD in ebay and local stores

Then, you have to install ST LINK UTILITY 
https://www.st.com/en/development-tools/stsw-link004.html

(You will need to create a free account to download it)

Once you have installed it, open ST LINK UTILITY.

Download the lastest version of the firmware (currently 1.3).

Connect the ST LINK gadget to a USB port of your computer. Then connect the gatget to your OPERA ROTAS following this schematic:

https://freeelectron.ro/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/connections.png

In ST LINK UTILITY open the firmware that you dowloaded (1.3), then press RESET button on the bluepill and while is pressed, click "PROGRAM VERIFY" on the ST LINK UTILY.

Release reset button. It should be uploading the new firmware.

Your OR is updated.
